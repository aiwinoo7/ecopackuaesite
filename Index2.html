About Us

ECOPACK LLC is one of the leading distributors of Hotel & Catering products based in UAE since 2015. We have specializes in offering tailored solutions to suit the tastes and preferences of our clients.

Since inception we have built up solid infrastructures, distribution mobility, warehousing facility and above all very strong and efficient marketing strategies. We have an established client services team, which can provide - an outsourcing, managed service to bind together numerous business functions. We hope to establish long term client relationships with, providing consist service levels.

  • Over the years we have launched and positioned many products in the market. Our vast product range, competitive price and excellent service have enabled us to establish, as a consistent supplier in this Industry.
  • Our expertise and long team experience in hospitality industry has enabled us to source and supply a wide range of Hotel & catering products which includes Housekeeping Products, F& B Service Items and Disposables. We also specialized in innovative and customized products that suit the unique needs of our client.
  • We have built our reputation in the market on being responsive, reliable and have formed longteam relationship with all our customers. Let us show you how we can make the usual tedious task of Hotel and Catering supply a simple and hassle-free experience.
